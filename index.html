Tee's ATL: 7 Days Done Right.

No tourist traps, just vibes. Here's how a week in Atlanta goes when your guide knows every layer of the city — the culture, the creativity, and the joy.

01 Welcome to ATL 🥂
Party Brunch & Afro-Caribbean Dinner
Garden Parc ATL — bottomless mimosas, live DJ & unlimited food Rock Steady — Afro-Caribbean cocktails & dinner vibes
02 Hidden Gems & Plantinis 🌱🍸
Wellness, Free Plants & Classy Dinner
iGetJuiced — best açai bowls in the city Seed ATL — espresso martinis + free plants (Plantini event) The Select ATL — ambient, classy, chef's kiss
03 Treat Yourself 💅🏽
Spa Day at the Four Seasons & Skyline Sushi
Four Seasons Spa — arguably the best spa experience in Atlanta O-Ku Atlanta — sushi, skyline views & curated cocktails
04 Nature & Nostalgia 🌳🎮
Nature, Black-Owned Coffee & Grown-Up Arcade
Cascade Springs Nature Preserve — scenic escape Black Coffee ATL — Black-owned & full of charm ✊🏾 Chattahoochee Food Works — food halls as a love language Your 3rd Spot — games, drinks & grown-up fun
05 Art & Tulum Energy 🎨🌮
90s R&B Paint Night & Hidden Gem Tacos
Dine & Design — paint & sip with a millennial, 90s R&B twist CT Taqueria (Gwinnett) — Tulum vibes without leaving the city
06 BBQ & Bougie 🍷🔥
Korean BBQ & Wine Country Golden Hour
THRILL Korean BBQ — bold energy, bolder flavors Château Élan — wine tasting, sunset views & content-worthy moments
07 Slow Sunday ☁️
Luxe Brunch, PCM Window Shopping & Cozy Wind-Down
Dirty Rascal — Italian-American brunch with big flavor & luxe décor Black Coffee Atlanta — one more for the road ✊🏾 Ponce City Market — light window shopping & city energy

About the Creator

Hi, I'm Tee.

"I realized I was constantly curating experiences, planning epic trips, and helping others find joy in the little things. That's when it clicked — I wasn't just sharing travel recs, I was building a lifestyle brand rooted in joy, ease, and intention."

I don't just share where to go — I share how it feels. Whether it's a Black-owned spa in Miami or a beachside resort in Cancún, my content makes travel feel like home and look like art. Wander & Wit is a movement for people — especially Black travelers — to feel seen, inspired, and reminded that they deserve beautiful experiences too.

📍 Based in Atlanta. Exploring everywhere.
